About Me

Hi! My name is Will, and this is my 2nd time pursuing post-secondary education. Before enrolling at Algonquin College, I studied computer science at Carleton University. I graduated in 2021, but I was unsuccessful in finding any work in my field of study. (To be honest, I realized that I was not interested in a career as a software developer.) Instead, I worked a variety of different jobs including warehouse operations and wireless sales. I am currently employed as a host at a local escape room facility.

Beware that, when fighting monsters, you yourself do not become a monster... for when you gaze long into the abyss. The abyss gazes also into you. Friedrich Nietzsche

I decided to return to school to gain some more employable skills and follow a new career path. I joined Algonquin's IMD program to develop the skills required for me to design, publish, and market my own TTRPG content. Ever since my first Dungeons & Dragons game at the age of 16, I have been a huge fan of the hobby. My dream is to make my living in the tabletop gaming industry both as a game designer and as a professional game master.
